Tator Tots

Description

Air Fryer Tater Tots. It’s super easy to air fry perfectly crisp and crunchy tots in less than 15 minutes! This no-fail recipe uses frozen tater tots and your favorite seasoning blends.

Ingredients

  • Frozen tater tots – no need to thaw!
  • Olive oil – or canola, avocado, or grapeseed oil
  • -Seasonings your choice, see suggestions below-
  • Lemon pepper seaononing, Old Bay, Creole, Adobo, Ranch seasoning.

Directions

  • First, preheat the air fryer to 400°F.
  • Set out a mixing bowl. Place the frozen tater tots in the bowl, and drizzle olive oil over the top.
  • Toss well. Then sprinkle with 1 teaspoon of your favorite salted seasoning blend.
  • Toss again to coat in seasoning.
  • Spread the tater tots across the bottom of the air fryer basket in an even layer. Be careful that they do not overlap!
  • Air fry for 6 minutes. Then gently shake the basket, and air fry for another 3 to 5 minutes.
  • When they are perfectly golden and crunchy, transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate.
